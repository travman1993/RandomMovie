Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

Movie Picker is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referrer information

Information You Provide

  • Contact form submissions (name, email, message)
  • Feedback and suggestions

We do NOT collect: Personal identification numbers, financial information, location data, or tracking cookies.

How We Use Your Information

  • To improve Movie Picker's functionality and user experience
  • To respond to your inquiries and feedback
  • To analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • To comply with legal obligations

Third-Party Services

Movie Picker may use third-party services for analytics and advertising. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them:

  • Google Analytics: Tracks anonymous usage data
  • Google AdSense: Displays relevant advertisements
  • Formspree: Processes contact form submissions

We do not share your personal information with these services beyond what is necessary for their operation.

Cookies

Movie Picker uses minimal cookies. Most of our analytics and advertising rely on anonymous tracking. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time without affecting core functionality.

Data Security

We take data security seriously. All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols. However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure. We do our best to protect your information, but cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking
  • Disable cookies in your browser

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@moviepicker.app

Children's Privacy

Movie Picker is not intentionally directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will immediately delete it.

Policy Changes

Movie Picker reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Modified" date. Your continued use of Movie Picker constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@moviepicker.app
  • Website: www.moviepicker.app
