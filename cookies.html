Cookies Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, mobile phone) when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferences or login status. Cookies are commonly used to enhance user experience and website functionality.

Cookies Used by Movie Picker

Movie Picker uses minimal cookies. Our primary reliance is on anonymous analytics and advertising services rather than direct cookie tracking. Here are the cookies we may use:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration Type
_ga Google Analytics - user identification 2 years Analytics
_gid Google Analytics - session tracking 24 hours Analytics
_gat Google Analytics - request throttling 1 minute Analytics
IDE Google AdSense - ad personalization 1 year Marketing

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. Without them, certain features may not work correctly. Examples include session cookies that maintain your login status.

Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with Movie Picker. This helps us improve the website and user experience. These cookies collect anonymous data about:

  • Number of visitors
  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on site
  • Device type and browser

Marketing Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display relevant advertisements. Marketing cookies may be used to track your interests and show ads that are more relevant to you across different websites.

Third-Party Cookies

Third-party services like Google may set their own cookies on your device. We do not control these cookies, but we encourage you to review their privacy policies.

How to Control Cookies

You have the right to control cookie usage on your device. Here are your options:

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through settings. You can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Accept only essential cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Receive a warning before a cookie is stored

Refer to your browser's help section for instructions specific to your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, etc.).

Opt-Out of Google Services

  • Google Analytics: Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Google Ads: Visit Google's Ads Settings page to personalize your ad preferences

Note About Disabling Cookies

Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of Movie Picker or other websites. Some features may not work as intended. However, core functionality (like picking a movie) will continue to work.

Cookie Consent

By continuing to use Movie Picker, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy. If you do not consent to cookie usage, please disable cookies in your browser settings or stop using the website.

Updates to This Policy

Movie Picker may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Modified" date. Your continued use of the website constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
